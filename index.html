who we are

Women in Sustainability Africa (WiSA) is a pan-African network and movement dedicated to empowering women who are driving change across the sustainability landscape. From grassroots organizers to corporate leaders, scientists, entrepreneurs, and policy advocates, we bring together diverse voices committed to creating a greener, fairer, and more inclusive Africa. Our work spans mentorship, advocacy, knowledge sharing, and community-building—ensuring that women are not only included in sustainability dialogues but are leading them. We believe that true sustainability cannot be achieved without gender equity, and that African women hold the key to unlocking innovative, culturally rooted, and transformative climate solutions

Mission Statement

Women in Sustainability Africa empowers, connects, and amplifies the voices of African women leading the way in environmental stewardship, climate resilience, and sustainable development. We are committed to fostering inclusive leadership, driving equitable green innovation, and building a more just and sustainable future for all across the continent.

Vision Statement

To see a thriving Africa where women are at the forefront of sustainability—shaping policies, leading innovation, and building resilient communities that honor both people and planet.
